# Organization settings

**Organization Settings > Automatic Children** is where you manage **plans**, **subscriptions**, and **licensed projects** for your Azure DevOps organization.

## Opening the hub

1. Open your **organization** (not just a project).
2. Go to **Organization Settings** (gear icon at org level).
3. Find **Automatic Children** in the hub list. Open it.

## Plans and pricing

- The hub shows available **plans** (e.g. Trial, Starter, Team, Business, Enterprise) and pricing.
- **Subscribe**, **Upgrade**, or **Downgrade** — Use the buttons or links to change plan. Checkout may open in an overlay or external page (e.g. Paddle).

## Manage subscription

- **Manage subscription** — Link to the publisher’s customer portal where you can update payment method, cancel, or change plan. Use this for billing and subscription lifecycle.

## Licensed projects

- Each plan has a **maximum number of licensed projects**. In this section you choose **which projects** in the organization use the subscription.
- Add or remove projects from the licensed list. Only projects in this list get the full extension beyond trial limits (if applicable). Other projects may run in trial mode (limited rules/cascades).

## Support and legal

- **Contact**, **Support**, **Privacy**, **Terms** — Links provided in the hub for support and legal pages. Use them for bugs, feature requests, and policy information.

For more on trial vs licensed and plan limits, see [Licensing](/concepts/licensing).
