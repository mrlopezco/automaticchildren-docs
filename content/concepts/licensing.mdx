# Licensing

Automatic Children offers a **trial** and several **paid plans**. Licensing is managed at the **organization** level; you choose which **projects** use the subscription.

## Trial

- On **trial**, you can enable a limited number of **rules** and **cascades** per project (e.g. up to 3 enabled rules and 3 enabled cascades). Exact limits are shown in **Project Settings > Automatic Children** (License / About).
- Trial does not require payment. To unlock more rules and cascades or to use the extension without limits on more projects, you can subscribe to a plan.

## Plans

Plans are configured in **Organization Settings > Automatic Children**. Typical plans include:

- **Trial** — Limited rules/cascades per project.
- **Starter** — For small teams.
- **Team** — For growing teams.
- **Business** — For larger organizations.
- **Enterprise** — For organization-wide use.

Each plan has a **maximum number of licensed projects**. You assign which projects in the organization use the subscription (e.g. “Licensed projects” checkboxes or list). Only those projects can use the full extension beyond trial limits.

## Managing your subscription

- **Subscribe / Upgrade / Downgrade** — Done from **Organization Settings > Automatic Children**, often via a checkout flow (e.g. Paddle).
- **Manage subscription** — A link (e.g. “Manage subscription”) opens the publisher’s customer portal where you can update payment or cancel.
- **Licensed projects** — In the same hub, you select which projects are licensed. Projects not in the list fall back to trial limits (if applicable).

## Where to see your status

- **Project level:** **Project Settings > Automatic Children** — About or License tab may show whether the project is on trial or licensed.
- **Organization level:** **Organization Settings > Automatic Children** — Plans, current subscription, and licensed projects list.

For pricing and plan details, use the links in the extension or the marketplace listing.
